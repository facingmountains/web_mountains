The personal and heartfelt music of Facing Mountains was born out of a longing from Dutch composer and songwriter Xander Slikker. After treading on numerous musical journeys, he developed an interest for new and old music. Eventually he has found a way to combine his love for classical, alternative and electronic music, and shape it into one identity.

A believe in the power of melodic storytelling is what lies behind and drives Facing Mountains. The fluid style evokes introspective images with it's strong influence of classical music and fine balance between acoustic and electronic sounds. With artists such as Gotye, Radiohead and Nick Drake as an inspiration, Facing Mountains creates rich and colourful alternative pop music with cinematic elements.

In 2020, after a year of isolation and devotion to working on new music, the first self-produced album 'Season's end' was released. This was a first step into a longer musical journey. Set to discover the expressive possibilities of story, melody and melancholy.
